Artrage

Artrage is a music producer wearing many hats, including this one. Someone said once, this looks so nineties. That was really funny! There is a lot of music to be made, and Artrage is ready.

This photo was taken in on February 15, 2019, in Dublin. Notice he is only wearing a T-shirt. That's not Photoshop. Sometimes it doesn't rain there, which is certainly inspiring.

Berlin

Berlin is a state of mind, and mine is permanent. It is the city that inspires and can give love if you can give love. Its vibe is otherwordly, yet it is all happening in ours.

Would be nice to live in Berlin for a while and make a record there. Been to so many gigs and shows there, feel so good taking a walk along the streets full of life.

World

The world is too big not to see and explore while it and us are alive. There is a bunch of stuff we do to our planet that are not awesome. Artrage is a big believer in doing good stuff though.

Sure, sounds pretentious, but this isn't Greta hype. This actually is a massive concern and a mission he is on. Well, without Greta, naturally. And our world is nice, isn't it?

Posing

Artrage isn't one for posing, but this picture was taken by his brother as they visited the glorious town of Lviv. Not exactly posing per se, but sure a good and unplanned photo opportunity.

Or maybe he is but there never is anyone with a camera around, and selfies don't really work. Regardless, he is a fan of nice interiors, impressive architecture and white sandy beaches and turquoise sea. No photos necessary.
